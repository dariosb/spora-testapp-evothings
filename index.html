    This app demonstrates the functionality of the DA1458x CodeLess firmware.


    The smartphone, or tablet, will operate as a CodeLess central, and will provide an interface to transmit AT commands and receive responses from a DA1458x running the CodeLess firmware


    Simply load the CodeLess firmware onto your device and use this app to scan, connect and communicate with it.


    Enjoy!


    Bluetooth scan time: 10 seconds
